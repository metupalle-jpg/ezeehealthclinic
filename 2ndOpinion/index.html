About the Service

An independent clinical review system for elective surgeries and procedures, designed for Thiqa insurance holders in Abu Dhabi. Combining UK clinical expertise with evidence-based guidelines to ensure appropriate surgical care.

NICE Guidelines

Every case reviewed against the latest National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE) clinical criteria for the specific procedure requested.

UK Consultant Doctors

Panel of UK-trained, GMC-registered consultant surgeons providing independent clinical opinions across bariatric, ophthalmology, ENT, spine, and cardiology.

AI-Powered Analysis

Automated rules engine extracts clinical data from uploaded documents and correlates against NICE guidelines, providing structured decision support.

How It Works

1

Upload Clinical Documents

Upload the patient's EMR records, lab results, imaging reports, and pre-authorisation request as PDF documents.

2

AI Document Processing

The system extracts and segments clinical data — demographics, diagnosis, investigations, medications, and treatment history.

3

Rules Engine Analysis

NICE guidelines are applied to the extracted data, checking each mandatory criterion for the requested procedure.

4

Clinical Decision

A structured recommendation is generated — approve, request more information, or refer for consultant review.
